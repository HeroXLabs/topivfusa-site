Requirements/Qualifications

The applicants must fulfill one of the following two requirements to be accepted as a candidate.

  • (1) A graduate from medical school, nursing school, medical technology department, or a graduate who majored in biology, biochemistry or other sciences related to medicine. Each applicant should have two-year experience in these fields after his/her graduation. After finishing the training program, the applicants will go back their home counties.
  • (2) An applicant who has had a business related to medical fields for at least two years. The applicant, showing great interest in reproductive medicine,wishes to promote Wang Tube System in his/her home country after he/she has finished the training. After finishing the training program, the applicants will go back their home counties.

VISA

A Special Training Visa (H3) may be issued by U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Services.
